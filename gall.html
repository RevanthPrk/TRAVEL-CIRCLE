HYDERABAD

Hyderabad is the capital and largest city of the Indian state of Telangana and de jure capital of Andhra Pradesh.Occupying 650 square kilometres (250 sq mi) along the banks of the Musi River, located on the Deccan Plateau in the northern part of South India.
